|Takeaways|
************

* Open source licenses govern the rules of changing and distributing code, and enable anyone to hack anything. However, culture strongly regulate who can do what.

* Consciously or not, open source culture is based on |Lock Property Theory|: labor leads to ownership. 

* Open source culture is a "gift culture".

* A conflict resolution system is critical to an open source project.
