Open Source History¶

Open source software has been a hot topic for over thirty years.

The rich history of open source software began with “hackers”, evolved into “free software”, then “open source.”

Readings¶

The best concise history is Eric S. Raymond’s A Brief History Hackerdom.

For more background, also see History of free and open-source software on Wikipedia and Revolution OS on YT.



Milestones in History¶

We will look at a small subset of open source milestones. There is much more to learn on your own.

  • 1985 - Free Software Foundation
  • 1991 - Linux
  • 1997 - The Cathedral and the Bazaar
  • 1998 - Netscape and the Open Source Initiative
  • 1999 - Apache Software Foundation
  • 2001 - Drupal
  • 2008 - GitHub
  • 2012 - edX
