Open Source Governance¶

Readings¶

  • How Do You Define Good Open Source Governance?

Takeaways¶

  • A benevolant dictator (BD) leads and facilitates the community. Only the BD merges code. The BD model is simple, but depends on the BD being neutral and respected.
  • A meritocracy does not have leaders. Decisions are made by action. Contributions earn merit and merit earns authority.
  • Main difference in models is how deadlocks are broken.
